Police catch Korean man running illegal football betting website in Vietnam

Tuesday, September 01, 2015, 11:47 GMT+7

Police in Ho Chi Minh City on Monday said they had broken up an illegal online football-betting ring run by a South Korean man to serve his compatriots.

Police Operation and Arrest of Lee Won Il

On August 26, after days of investigation, police in District 7, Ho Chi Minh City, raided a house in the Sky Garden 1 area in Tan Phong Ward, catching Lee Won Il, a 32-year-old South Korean, operating an illegal online football betting website, www.CL-xxxx.com. Lee had set up the website to serve South Koreans betting on football matches and earned a 10% commission on each wager, which was sent to his bank account in South Korea. The police seized two CPUs and four computer monitors from him.

Related Investigation in Nha Be District

On August 29, police in Nha Be District inspected house No. 77 in the Ngan Long villa area, Phuoc Kieng Ward, and found 13 South Koreans residing without temporary residence registration. Police suspect that these individuals were involved in illegal football betting activities connected to the website run by Lee. The investigation into their possible involvement is ongoing.

Illegal Football Betting in Vietnam

Football betting is prohibited in all forms in Vietnam. The authorities treat such activities as illegal, with serious legal consequences for those found engaging in or organizing betting operations. Lee Won Il's website was specifically designed to cater to South Koreans living in Vietnam, highlighting the cross-border nature of the operation.

Previous Case of South Koreans Involved in Betting

In a similar case on November 6, 2014, Ho Chi Minh City police fined 14 South Korean nationals for organizing illegal football gambling. Fines ranged from VND10 million ($445) to VND20 million ($890) under Article 26 of Government Decree 167/2013, and the immigration authorities were instructed to expel them from the country. This shows a precedent for harsh penalties against foreign nationals involved in such illegal activities in Vietnam.
