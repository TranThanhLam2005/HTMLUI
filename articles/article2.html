Police crackdown on football gambling

Wednesday, 17:13, 10/07/2024

The Criminal Police Department under the Ministry of Public Security stated on July 10 that they collaborated with police of the southern province of Khanh Hoa to smash a huge online betting ring for matches at the ongoing Euro 2024 and Copa America competitions, with transactions amounting to over VND500 billion.

Collaboration in Cracking Down on the Betting Ring

On July 10, the Criminal Police Department, in collaboration with the Khanh Hoa Provincial Police, successfully dismantled a massive online betting operation centered around the Euro 2024 and Copa America football competitions. The gambling ring had transactions totaling over VND500 billion. The operation targeted football matches and was carried out through the website Bong88.xxx, one of the most prominent illegal betting platforms in the case.

Cao Dinh Hung as the Mastermind

The mastermind behind the betting ring was identified as Cao Dinh Hung, 33, residing in Phuoc Long ward, Nha Trang City. Since February, Hung had been managing a Super Master account on the Bong88.xxx platform, which facilitated the placement of bets from various gamblers. His account allowed him to control and supervise all betting activities linked to the platform. This central role made him a critical target in the police investigation.

Arrest of Additional Suspects

In addition to Cao Dinh Hung, police arrested 19 other suspects involved in the gambling operation. These individuals had different roles in managing the betting process, assisting with transactions, and maintaining the network of gamblers. Their arrests were part of a coordinated effort by law enforcement to dismantle the entire operation and prevent further illegal gambling activity.

Gambling Laws in Vietnam and Ongoing Investigations

In Vietnam, most forms of gambling, including sports betting, are illegal, with a few exceptions such as state-run lotteries. The police continue to investigate the case to identify any further participants or operations related to the betting ring. This crackdown reflects the government's ongoing efforts to combat illegal gambling, which is seen as a serious issue that can lead to financial crimes and social unrest.
