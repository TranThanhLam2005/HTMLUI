Six arrested for running football gambling website

28 Jun 2024 at 20:16

Site that took bets on Euro 2024 matches had B161 million in turnover, say police

Police Raids and Arrests in Connection with Autowin888

Police conducted raids on four locations in Chiang Rai and Pathum Thani provinces, resulting in the arrest of six individuals involved in running an online gambling website, Autowin888. The website was used to receive bets on Euro 2024 football matches, and had a turnover of 161 million baht. The raids, led by Pol Maj Gen Athip Pongsiwapai, commander of the Technology Crime Suppression Division (TCSD), targeted three sites in Chiang Rai and one in Pathum Thani.

Identities of the Arrested Suspects

The six suspects arrested during the raids were identified as four women and two men: Patcha Natesungnoen, 41; Patthraporn Saemon, 25; Sudarat Kerdkaewmuangmul, 36; Wannapha Thephakhum, 28; Chaichanadet Jantakhad, 29; and Kawinphat Sirisapyakhun, 26. All suspects faced charges of operating online gambling and laundering money, as outlined in warrants issued by the Criminal Court.

Seizure of Evidence During Raids

Police confiscated various items during the raids, including two computers, 29 mobile phones, 22 bank passbooks, 19 ATM cards, two cars, 3.6 million baht in cash, two land title deeds, a shotgun, and other valuable assets. These items were believed to be linked to the illegal gambling operation and were seized as part of the investigation.

Details of the Operation and Suspects' Confessions

Autowin888 was a highly active gambling site, with operations stretching from October last year to April this year, generating a turnover of 161 million baht. According to police, the site’s owner, Mr. Kawinphat Sirisapyakhun, previously worked for a gambling website in Myanmar before establishing his own site in July of the previous year. All six suspects admitted to the charges, and they are currently in police custody, awaiting further legal action. Police have increased their scrutiny of gambling websites, particularly during the Euro tournament, as the surge in online betting activities continues.
